üìà Progressive Overload Rules

The Golden Rule: When you can comfortably complete all sets and reps for an exercise at the target RPE, increase the weight by the smallest available increment in the next session.

Progression Guidelines:

  • Month 1: Focus on form mastery, small increments (1-2,5 kg upper body, 2,5-5 kg lower body)
  • Month 2: Prioritize progressive overload, larger increments (2,5-5 kg upper body, 5-7 kg lower body)
  • Month 3: Push intensity while maintaining form, focus on intensity techniques
